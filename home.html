How to Use This Site?

3 easy steps:

  • Choose Age Group

What is Jesus Saves?

Jesus Saves was created to help share the Gospel and lead others to Christ through a virtual platform. It features different illustrations of the Gospel to clearly present the message of salvation. The website can be used in online settings, shared through messaging apps as a digital tract, or even shown in person during conversations.
