Life After Death

Death is an event we cannot avoid, so it is necessary to prepare for it.

This website will teach you to ensure heaven according to the Word of God.

There are five things in the Bible you need to know:

I. WE ARE ALL SINNERS
Romans 3:23 KJV — "For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God."

Because of our sins we are separated from God.

II. SIN HAS A PAYMENT
Romans 6:23 KJV — "For the wages of sin is death..."

Sin requires the payment of death. The Bible teaches two types of death: physical and spiritual.

Revelation 21:8 KJV — "... and all liars, shall have their part in the lake which burneth with fire and brimstone: which is the second death."

If physical death is the separation of the soul from the body, spiritual death is the separation of the soul from God and its going to hell, a place of eternal fire, punishment, and suffering.

Sin has a penalty of death in Hell. The Bible tells us that wealth, religion, and good works cannot save us from hell.

III. JESUS PAID FOR US
Romans 5:8 KJV — "But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us."

Someone has already paid for our sins, the Lord Jesus. He was crucified, died, was buried, and rose again to pay the penalty we owe for our sins in hell.

We no longer need to work for our salvation or add to what Christ has done because He Himself said in John 19:30, "It is finished". Because of what Jesus did, salvation has become a gift that everyone must accept.

Ephesians 2:8-9 — "For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast."
Romans 6:23 — "... but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord."
IV. BELIEVE IN JESUS

God wants to save you now and give you the gift of eternal life, if you will only believe in Jesus and accept Him as your personal Savior.

John 3:16 — "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life."
Romans 10:13 — "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved."

Accept Jesus Christ as your own personal Savior today through prayer. But this prayer itself cannot save you — only your faith in Jesus can.

Prayer:
Lord God, I confess to you that I am a sinner and I repent of all my sins. Please forgive me, and I believe that Jesus paid for all my sins. I now accept Jesus Christ into my heart as my personal Savior and Lord of my life. Amen.

V. ASSURANCE OF HEAVEN
Romans 8:1 — "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus..."

You're bound for heaven, not hell.

John 10:28 — "And I give unto them eternal life; and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand."

The salvation you've received can never be lost.
